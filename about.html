About Bonn Cargo featuring F1

Virtual Group

Experience the thrilling world of Formula 1 logistics through flight simulation. We bring the high-stakes world of motorsport cargo operations to virtual pilots worldwide.

Our Mission

Bonn Cargo was founded to give flight simulation enthusiasts a unique opportunity to participate in one of the most complex logistics operations in the world. Every Formula 1 season, thousands of tonnes of equipment must be transported to circuits across five continents, often with just days between races. We simulate these operations, giving pilots the chance to fly the same routes that keep the world's most prestigious motorsport championship running.

How It Works

Real Routes

Our routes are based on real-world F1 logistics patterns, flying from DHL's global hubs to circuit airports around the world.

Race Calendar

Our schedule follows the official F1 calendar, with cargo flights timed to arrive before each race weekend.

Diverse Cargo

From race cars and pit equipment to broadcast gear and hospitality supplies - experience the full range of F1 logistics.

Community

Join fellow virtual pilots who share a passion for both aviation and motorsport in our growing community.

Our Fleet

We operate a fleet of wide-body cargo aircraft, modeled after the real aircraft used in F1 logistics operations:

  • Boeing 777F - Long-haul cargo operations, 103 tonnes capacity
  • Airbus A330-300 - Medium-haul freight, 70 tonnes capacity
  • Boeing 767-300F - Short-haul freight, 52 tonnes capacity
